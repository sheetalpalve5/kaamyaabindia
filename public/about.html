About Us

Who are we?

KaamYaab India is a one stop common platform accessible to all which focusses solely for the benifit of workers and daily wage earners who have been impactted due to Covid-19 crisis or otherwise. Registered users are provided with option of employment opportunitites available based on their skills by connecting them with emploers in need to hire skill based employees. We aim to help workers find jobs per their skills and location, faciliate employers find resources per their demand, provide information on financial options available for workers in this crisis and provide resources of virtual trainings for them to learn and enhance their skills in order to enhance their options.

Our Goal

Due to Covid-19 crisis, a lot of underpriviledged members of our society have suffered a serious setback in income and had to either go back to their hometowns to sustain themselves and their families, or have to rely on external aids. In the new normal post Covid, we as a society face a major challenge to our fellow indians get back on their feet and re-empower them by finding suitable employment opportunitites as per their skills and experience.

Our Impact

Job Portals that exists today focuss mainly on corporate world. The employment assistance platforms for blue collared jobs remains in silos and disconnected. This one stop platform aims to ease the process for the workers directly as well as aim to help NGO's and community bodies working to help them on their behalf.
